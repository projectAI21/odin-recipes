Chicken Noodles

Description

Chicken noodles are a delicious mix of boiled noodles stir-fried with seasoned chicken, vegetables, and sauces. Itâ€™s a favorite in Indo-Chinese cuisine, known for its spicy and savory flavors.

Perfect as a main course or quick dinner, these noodles are satisfying, flavorful, and easily customizable.

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles
  • 200g boneless chicken (sliced)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, capsicum, cabbage)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil noodles as per package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok and stir-fry chicken until cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. Stir-fry onions and vegetables until slightly tender.
  4. Add cooked chicken, noodles, and all sauces. Mix well.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and toss everything together.
  6. Serve hot with chili vinegar or soy sauce on the side.
