Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a quick and satisfying dish made with pre-cooked rice stir-fried with vegetables, eggs, and optional proteins like chicken or shrimp. It's a versatile Asian dish often made with leftover rice.

It's flavorful, colorful, and can be customized with whatever ingredients you have at hand. Soy sauce and sesame oil give it a classic umami taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok. Scramble eggs and set aside.
  2. Saut√© onions and vegetables until tender.
  3. Add the rice and mix well.
  4. Stir in soy sauce, sesame oil, and scrambled eggs.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Cook for another 3-4 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite side.
