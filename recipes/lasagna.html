Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian layered pasta dish made with sheets of pasta, rich meat or veggie sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's baked to perfection for a gooey, golden-brown top.

This comforting dish is great for family dinners, special occasions, or meal prepping ahead. It's filling, flavorful, and universally loved.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef or chicken
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles and set aside.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic, add meat, cook until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and seasoning. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with parmesan and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes.
  6. Let it cool slightly before serving.
